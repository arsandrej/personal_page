Games

Batman Arkham Trilogy

The Batman Arkham trilogy holds a special place in my heart. These games offer an immersive experience that captures the essence of being the Dark Knight. Each game in the trilogy – Arkham Asylum, Arkham City, and Arkham Knight – builds on its predecessor, creating a rich, interconnected storyline that keeps you engaged from start to finish.

My favourite aspect of this game is how it combines stealth, combat, and detective work in a thrilling narrative. The voice acting, particularly Mark Hamill's Joker and Kevin Conroy's Batman, adds depth and authenticity to the characters.

Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima, is another favorite that stands out for its breathtaking visuals and engaging gameplay. Set in feudal Japan, the game follows Jin Sakai, a samurai warrior fighting to liberate the island of Tsushima from Mongol invaders.

The narrative is compelling, depicting Jin's struggle between honoring the samurai code and adopting unconventional tactics to save his homeland. The character development is rich, with memorable supporting characters.

Ghost of Tsushima isn’t just a game; it’s an experience. Its blend of history, culture, and engaging gameplay creates a journey that’s both emotionally impactful and incredibly fun to play.
